Tic Tac Toe - Written using AngularJS

I wrote this application to teach myself AngularJS. The game is very well-known - at least in Western countries - every schoolchild learns it. And it's probably some sort of early developmental milestone to realize that played properly, it is virtually impossible to lose. Thus, I think most of us lose interest in it as it's a game notorious for draws.
